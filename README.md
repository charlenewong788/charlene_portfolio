# Charlene Portfolio

A personal portfolio website for Charlene, an MSc Light and Lighting student at University College London. The site introduces my academic background, research interests, education, experience, and contact information, with a focus on lighting design, indoor environmental quality, daylight, wellbeing, and environmental design research.

[https://charlenewong788.github.io/charlene_portfolio/](https://charlenewong788.github.io/charlene_portfolio/)

## About

This portfolio is designed as a clean and professional personal homepage. It presents:

- A short personal introduction
- Academic background in Light and Lighting and Electrical & Electronic Engineering
- Education and professional experience
- A research framework covering lighting, wellbeing, emotion, interaction, and simulation
- Contact links, including email, LinkedIn, and GitHub

## Research Focus

The portfolio highlights four connected areas:

- **Engineering Foundation**: Electrical engineering, measurement systems, and analytical thinking
- **Lighting MSc Training**: Visual comfort, wellbeing, and sustainable lighting design
- **Research Experience**: Smart classroom daylight optimisation, daylight provision, glare reduction, and student wellbeing
- **Digital Skills**: Rhino, Grasshopper, Auto CAD, SketchUp, simulation modelling, environmental analysis, and sensor data analysis
